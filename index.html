Life Flower
Interactive Design Project
This project visualizes the passage of time and life through an interactive kinetic sculpture that combines heart rate data, mechanical flowers, LED lights, and a clock. The blooming and wilting flowers symbolize life's cycles, while the ticking clock reflects the unstoppable flow of time. Light represents growth, hope, and the influence of culture and time on our experiences. The piece invites reflection on the fleeting nature of time, urging viewers to consider its preciousness and how it shapes their lives.
